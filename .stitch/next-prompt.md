**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**

Primary action: gradient green→blue linear-gradient(135deg, #068140, #00558F). Cards: white bg, subtle shadow, 16px radius, green top-border on feature cards. Stats/CTA/Footer: solid navy #002747.
